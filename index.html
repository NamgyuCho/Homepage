About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Korea University, advised by Prof. Seong-Whan Lee (and Prof. Alan Yuille for the first three years). My research interests are computer vision and machine learning. My current focus is applying deep learning for remote sening image understanding. Previously I worked on human behavior understanding including 3D human pose tracking, individual action and group activity recognition.
